### 2. twitter-image-nunex.jpg
- **Purpose**: Twitter Card image for Twitter/X sharing

- **Description**: Should represent NUNEX's brand as a Financial Consultancy and Blockchain ecosystem

The images should include:
- NUNEX logo prominently displayed
- Key messaging: Trading, Financiamiento, Blockchain
- Professional, corporate aesthetic
- Chilean/Argentine market appeal
- Clear, readable text even when scaled down
